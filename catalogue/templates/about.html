About Us

The SANSA Earth Observation & Space Operations Centre is a key component of SANSA's efforts to maximise the benefit of information, communications and space technology for industry and society. The centre at Hartebeesthoek is located some 70 km west of Pretoria in the Magaliesberg mountain range and is ideally positioned to provide tracking, telemetry and command (TT&C) services for geo-synchronous and polar orbiting spacecraft to the manufacturers, operators and users of satellites and launch vehicles. It is also ideally situated for satellite data acquisition and as such, delivers world-class earth observation data relayed from satellites to a range of stakeholders. It provides key remote sensing technologies, infrastructure, services and spatial information to address inter alia the mitigation of natural disasters; public safety and security; health-related issues; infrastructure and utilities; environmental conservation and effective land cover and land use classifications.

You can find more information about SANSA at our home page
