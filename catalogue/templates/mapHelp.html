Using the map

Use the zoom out and zoom in icons to zoom in and out on the map by dragging rectangles.

Use the pan icon on the map to enable map panning by dragging the map.

Use to restore and fullscreen icons to quickly resize map window. Restore will resize map to original size, while fullscreen will resize map to fill current browser window.

When the pan tool is enabled, and you are viewing search results, you can click on the yellow image footprint in the map to see its preview in the preview area.

When searching

Digitising an area of interest is not required but is recommended. Draw an area of interest on the map to refine the set of search results to a specific area.

Use the capture icon on the map to your left to draw an area of interest for your search.

Use the edit icon on the map to your left to adjust your area of interest for your search.

Keyboard shortcuts

Use the 'd' or 'delete' key (while holding the mouse cursor over it) to delete a vertex when editing the area of interest.

Use shift while dragging a rectangle on the map to zoom in to that rectangle.
