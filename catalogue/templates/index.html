Welcome to the SANSA Earth Observation Online Catalogue

The development of this catalogue was funded by the South African Department of Science and Technology.

This site provides the SANSA (South African National Space Agency) earth observation data catalogue for Landsat 4,5 MSS; Landsat 5 TM; Landsat 7 ETM; SPOT 1,2,4; ERS 1,2 and NOAA sensors. SANSA (and in their former capacity as a part of the CSIR) has been acquiring satellite earth observation data since the early 1980's when reception of Landsat-2 MSS data commenced.

A note on browser compatibility: This web site works with Mozilla Firefox 3 or better, recent versions of Google Chrome, Opera, Safari and Internet Explorer 8. Users of older versions of Internet Explorer should upgrade to version 8 to get the best experience.

Legacy Catalogue: The old catalogue (that this catalogue aims to replace) is still online here.
