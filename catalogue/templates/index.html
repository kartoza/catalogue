Welcome to the SANSA Earth Observation Online Catalogue

The development of this catalogue was funded by the Department of Science and Technology.

This site will become the main SANSA (South African National Space Agency) earth observation data catalogue for Landsat 4,5 MSS; Landsat 5 TM; Landsat 7 ETM; SPOT 1,2,4; ERS 1,2 and NOAA sensors. The CSIR has been acquiring satellite earth observation data since the early 1980's when reception of Landsat-2 MSS data commenced.

Browser compatibility: This web site works with Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, Opera, Safari and Internet Explorer 8. future.Users of older versions of Internet Explorer should upgrade to version 8 to get the best experience.

Legacy Catalogue: The old catalogue (that this catalogue aims to replace) is still online here.
